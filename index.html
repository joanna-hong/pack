Getting started

What is Pack?

Pack is a members' club for men 60+ who want to do more with good company. You can browse activities, join the ones that interest you, and stay in touch with men you meet along the way.

Do I need to bring friends with me?

No. Start by choosing an activity that interests you. After you register, we open a group chat with you and the other men signed up for it. From there it is up to you — start a conversation, attend another activity with the same group, or leave. No pressure, no long-term commitment.

Do I have to join a group right away?

No — you join a Pack only after you have attended an activity. Start with one activity; afterwards, your group appears in My Packs.

What does "My Packs" mean?

My Packs is the page where you see your activity groups and open their chat rooms. A Pack is a group of members connected through an activity — not something you have to create or manage yourself.

Where is Pack available?

We are starting in New York City. As we grow, we will add more neighborhoods and cities.

Membership & payment

Is Pack free to join?

Yes. There is no membership or subscription fee.

What do I pay for?

You only pay for paid activities you choose to attend. Community-led activities are mostly free, though some may have an activity cost (for example a movie ticket or food). Partner-led activities have a fee — a class, ticket, meal, rental, or materials cost.

Are there hidden fees?

No. The price is shown before you sign up for an activity.

Can I browse before creating an account?

Yes. You can look through activities first. You will need an account when you are ready to sign up.

Can I close my account?

Yes. You can close your account at any time.

Activities

What kinds of activities are offered?

Build & Restore · Food, Drink & Table · Field & Water · Games & Strategy · Music & Nights Out · Culture & Arts · Masterclasses & Skills · Build & Give Back · Road & Voyage.

Are activities only active or outdoors?

No. We offer active, easygoing, seated, and virtual activities. You can choose what fits you.

What do the activity levels mean?

Active: more walking, movement, or time on your feet. Easygoing: a slower pace, such as a short walk, meal, tasting, or museum visit. Seated: take part while sitting most of the time. Virtual: join online from home.

How do I find the right activity?

Use the filters on the Activities page — price, activity level, category, date, and location.

Can I attend an activity alone?

Yes. Signing up by yourself is a normal way to start.

Who runs the activities?

Some activities are community-led; others are run by vetted local partners. The activity page shows who is hosting.

What if I sign up and cannot attend?

You can cancel from the activity page. For paid activities, the cancellation policy is shown before you sign up.

Creating an activity

Can I suggest my own activity?

Yes. Use the "Submit your own activity" page on the Activities tab to send us your idea.

What kinds of activities can I submit?

Any idea that fits the categories — a walk, lunch, fishing trip, museum visit, card game, workshop, class, or local outing.

Do I have to lead the activity myself?

No. An activity can be led by you or another member.

What happens after I submit an activity?

We review the idea. If it is approved, we help set it up and add it to the activities list for others to join. Someone from Pack may reach out with a few questions.

Will every submitted activity be approved?

Most are accepted. We review each idea for fit, safety, timing, and whether it can be organized clearly.

My Packs

What is a Pack?

A Pack is a group of members connected through an activity. After you register, the Pack appears on your My Packs page with a group chat for that activity.

What is the chat for?

Simple planning and staying in touch — confirm details, ask where to meet, get to know each other, plan another activity, or stay connected afterward.

Do I have to participate in the chat?

No. The chat is optional. Use it as much or as little as you want.

What if I do not have a Pack yet?

My Packs stays empty until you register for an activity.

Can I be in more than one Pack?

Yes. If you attend different activities with different groups, more than one Pack will appear.

Can I leave a Pack?

Yes. You are never tied to a group. You can leave a Pack or stop taking part in its chat.

Privacy & contact information

Can other members see my phone number or email?

No. Other members do not see your phone number or email through the site. You can choose to share contact information yourself.

Who can see my profile?

Other members can see basic profile information needed for activities and chat. Your private account information is not shown.

Do activity hosts see my information?

If you sign up, the host receives what is needed to run the activity, such as your name and attendance. We do not share more than needed.

Profile & verification

Why do I need a profile?

It helps us suggest activities that fit your interests, location, schedule, and preferred pace. Keep it simple at first and update it later.

What information do I need to join?

Your name, email, ZIP code, age, activity interests, preferred activity level, and general availability.

Why do you ask for my age?

This community is designed for men 60+. Asking for age helps us keep it focused.

Can I change my answers later?

Yes. You can update your profile and preferences from the Profile page.

What is verification?

A one-time identity check that helps keep the community safe and real.

When do I need to verify?

You can browse and create an account first. You verify from your Profile before joining an activity.

Will other members see my ID?

No. Other members will only see that you are verified.

Safety

How do you help keep members safe?

Member verification, vetted partners, verified hosts, clear activity details, and community rules. Members can also report concerns.

Is the chat moderated?

We review chats and step in when needed to keep the community respectful and safe.

What if I feel uncomfortable at an activity?

You can leave at any time, and report the concern to us afterward.

Can a member be removed?

Yes. We remove members who break community rules or create safety concerns.

Help using the site

Do I need to download an app?

No. There is no app right now. You can use the website on a phone, tablet, or computer.

Do I need to be good with technology?

No. The site is designed to be simple — browse activities, sign up, and check your profile without many steps.

What if I forget how to sign in?

We send you a sign-in link by email. There is no complicated password to remember.

Can someone help me sign up?

Yes. A family member or friend can help you create an account, as long as you want to join.

How do I contact you?

Email us at pack.helpteam@gmail.com, or use the Contact us button at the bottom of this page. You can ask a question, report an issue, or get help with your account.

The easiest way to start

What should I do first?

Browse the Activities page and choose one activity that looks interesting. That is enough for a first step.
