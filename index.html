How it works

How a Pack comes together.

1

Tell us what you enjoy

A few quick questions — what you'd like to do, how active, how near, and when you're free. That's it.

2

Come to something

We show you a handful of good things nearby. Pick one and go — no commitment.

3

Sort the basics first

A quick group chat handles the logistics — who's coming, where, when, what to bring, a lift if needed. No deep conversation; just so no one walks in cold.

4

Do the thing, shoulder to shoulder

Short and task-led to start. You're often paired with one settled member on the job — connection follows the work, not small talk.

5

Your Pack forms

From the men you keep seeing and get on with, we suggest a Pack — a group of about six — that keeps meeting.

6

Keep it going — the Pack rate

Your Pack meets regularly and rotates through new activities. Book as a Pack and you get the members' rate — and the bigger your Pack, the more you save (up to 10%).

Can I leave a Pack?

The Pack rate

Can my Pack get the rate more than once?

Yes — you get the Pack rate every time, even doing the same activity with the same group again and again, as long as you sign up as a Pack.
